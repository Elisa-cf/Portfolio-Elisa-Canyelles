What I do

Front-End

As a dedicated Frontend Developer, I craft captivating web applications using React.js and Vue.js. Back-End

Powered by Node.js, I deliver efficient and scalable applications, while harnessing the capabilities of AWS and Firebase for robust and reliable backend infrastructure.

Workflow

General info & mission of the project:

As an integral part of a transformational and cross-functional team, I've been involved in the end-to-end development of a ground-breaking product for Bunte.de magazine called Bunte Moments, a web and mobile app where influencers can upload their content and the users of Bunte.de can enjoy it.

The MVP (minimum viable product)

During the development of the MVP, I worked with only one other developer so I had a lot of responsibilities and I had to work independently taking care of many tasks related to the implementation of the frontend part of the web using Vue.js and of the mobile app using dart together with Flutter.

Daily Tasks after the MVP:

Now that we've successfully launched the MVP and the Team is growing, my daily tasks revolve around implementing new features, addressing bugs, and conducting thorough testing for both the web and app with each new release.
